Continuous Monitoring

Onboarding doesn't end at approval.

FleetAxis watches every approved carrier 24/7. The minute a carrier's authority lapses, insurance expires, or FMCSA flips a safety rating — your team gets pinged. Before a load gets booked. Before a claim happens.

  • Live FMCSA sync. Authority status, safety rating, OOS rate — checked hourly.
  • Insurance heartbeat. Direct underwriter integration. Cancellation notifications in real time.
  • Identity drift alerts. Phone, address, EIN, or contact name changes flagged immediately.
  • CSA score deltas. Intervention thresholds, BASIC scores, recent inspections.
  • Network signals. If another broker on FleetAxis flags a carrier, your team finds out.
