A note on insurance referrals
Insurance is regulated at the state level. FleetAxis lists insurance providers as an unpaid directory — we do not collect referral commissions on insurance products. Insurance partners may participate in the directory and benefit from co-marketing, but no monetary referral fee is paid or accepted. This protects everyone: carriers, brokers, and the providers themselves. Other categories (factoring, ELD, fuel, drug consortiums, CDL training, permits, accounting, legal) are not subject to the same restrictions, and standard commissions apply.
