Broker Lookup

Know who you're hauling for.

Every broker on FleetAxis comes with a real payment history — pulled from anonymous, verified data across our carrier network. Skip the no-pay brokers. Spot the slow-pays before you accept the load.

  • Days-to-pay history. Median, average, and worst-case pay times by broker.
  • BMC-84 / BMC-85 bond status. Live from FMCSA. Know if the bond is current before you book.
  • Identity verification. EIN match, registered agent, business address validated.
  • Network signals. No-pay flags, rate-con disputes, double-brokering history.
  • Carrier reviews. Verified ratings from real carriers who hauled real loads.
